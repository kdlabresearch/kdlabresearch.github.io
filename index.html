Кeen Dynamics Lab

Research analytic-lab center

About us

The Keen Dynamics Lab Center conducts laboratory research in the following areas:
PVT studies of gas-condensate systems;
PVT studies of deep samples of oil and water;
Chromatographic studies;
Determination of physicochemical properties of surface samples of oil, gas condensate, and gas;
Determination of commercial characteristics of oil and gas condensate;
Research of petroleum products;
Research of soils for engineering surveys and construction work.

Directions of research

The center of PVT-research

7 years of experience in conducting PVT studies of reservoir fluids. The laboratory of thermodynamic research evaluates the representativeness of samples at the sampling temperature. Four PVT setups from Sanchez Technologies (France), Vinchi Technologies (France), and Chandler Engineering (USA) are used for gas-condensate thermodynamic research in the Laboratory-Analytical Center (LAC). All four setups have high-pressure cells with maximum volumes of more than 1,000 cm³ equipped with mechanical pistons that provide precise control over the sample volume.

The center of chromatography research

The chromatographic research laboratory is engaged in determining the component-fractional composition of natural gas and liquid hydrocarbon systems (condensate, oil):
- Determination of the gas component composition up to C17+;
- Determination of the component composition of oil and gas condensate up to C36+;
- Determination of hydrogen sulfide and sulfur-containing components.

Laboratory of physical and chemical research

Performs the following types of research:

- Research of separated crude oil in accordance with the standard OST 153-39.2-048-2003;
- Extended research of crude oil, stable gas condensate with distillation into narrow fractions according to ASTM 2892-18a, determination of physico-chemical characteristics of narrow fractions, construction of true boiling temperature (TBP) curves, quality curves;
- Determination of group hydrocarbon and structural-group compositions in wide fractions;
- Determination of commercial characteristics of feedstock crude oil;
- Research of petroleum products for compliance with GOST, TU;
- Non-standard research of hydrocarbon systems.
